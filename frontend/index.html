About Us

At RND Servicing, we prioritise your indoor air quality with unwavering commitment. With extensive experience in the ventilation industry, we offer top-tier solutions for domestic and commercial customers alike. Our mission is to enhance the quality of your living and working environment through innovative ventilation services tailored to your needs. Our team consists of highly trained and qualified technicians who are dedicated to delivering exceptional service. We understand the importance of maintaining optimal indoor air quality, and we work tirelessly to ensure your ventilation systems are operating at their best. Whether it’s routine maintenance, system upgrades, or emergency repairs, you can count on us to provide reliable and efficient service every time.

Our Services

We offer tailored technical and mechanical ventilation services to meet your unique needs. Our services include MVHR (Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery) system maintenance, cleaning, and repairs, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency. We also specialise in the installation and commissioning of new systems, helping you create a healthier and more comfortable indoor environment. Additionally, we provide professional ventilation duct cleaning to remove dust, allergens, and contaminants, improving air quality and system efficiency. No matter the size or complexity of the project, we have the expertise and equipment to get the job done right. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how we can help improve your indoor air quality.
